About

Have you ever been looking for help trying to solve a statistics problem, but all of the online calculators you found were too difficult to use or required signing up for full access? Have you ever been looking for a clear explanation on probability distributions, but all of the websites you found gave you tons of ads and other unnecessary information? Look no further than SimpleStats!

On SimpleStats, there are calculators for almost any distribution you would encounter in an introductory statistics course. If you have difficulty understanding a specific distribution, there are help pages for each linked directly on the calculator's page. Or, if you need more general help using the website, visit the general help page. All of the calculators are free to use by anyone, any time they want.

No ads. No payment. No sign up. Just statistics. The way it should be.
